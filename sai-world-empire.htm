Overview

A mega township designed on the lifestyles of 6 famous kings & queens

  • Phase 1 OC received - 6 magnificent towers (Tower 1 - Caeser, Tower 2 - Alexander, Tower 3 - Napoleon, Tower 4 - Cleopatra, Tower 5 - Charles, Tower 6 - Elizabeth)
  • G+49 storeyed, 2, 3 & 4 BHK Homes.
  • Luxury Community
  • Grand clubhouse
  • Designer podium garden
  • Ample parking spaces

Project Highlights

INSPIRED BY THE ROYAL LIVES OF THE WORLD'S GREATEST KINGS & QUEENS
  • ARCH-DE-TRIUMPH ENTRANCE - The iconic gateway inspired by the glorious era of French Kingdom
  • THE BROADWAY - The signature amphitheatre inspired by British Broadway
  • THE ATHENA CLUBHOUSE - The marquee clubhouse inspired by the legacy and the grandeur of the Greek Kingdom
  • ASWAN SPA - A signature spa experience that will be designed based on ancient Egyptian Spas
  • ESPANA KIDS PARK - The children's theme park inspired by the Spanish Kingdom's amazing Gaudi architecture
